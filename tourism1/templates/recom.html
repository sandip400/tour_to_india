Hotel Recommendations for Himachal Trip

Shimla (Day 1 to Day 3)

Luxury Options:
The Oberoi Cecil, Shimla

Historic luxury hotel offering colonial charm, heated swimming pool, spa, and fine dining.

Wildflower Hall, An Oberoi Resort

Luxury resort nestled amidst cedar forests with mountain views, spa, and adventure activities.

Mid-range Options:
Hotel Willow Banks

Comfortable stay located on Mall Road offering modern amenities and city views.

Clarkes Hotel, Shimla

Charming heritage hotel offering traditional hospitality, central location, and elegant rooms.

Stay Duration: 3 Nights

Manali (Day 4 to Day 7)

Luxury Options:
The Himalayan

Castle-themed luxury resort featuring Gothic architecture, lush gardens, and stunning views.

Span Resort & Spa

Riverside resort offering luxurious cottages, spa services, and a peaceful natural environment.

Manuallaya - The Resort Spa in the Himalayas

Beautiful resort featuring a spa, indoor pool, and breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains.

Mid-range Options:
Honeymoon Inn Manali

Cozy hotel with vibrant rooms, perfect for couples and families looking for a peaceful stay.

Johnson Lodge & Spa

Charming lodge offering stylish rooms, a popular cafe, and rejuvenating spa services.

Stay Duration: 3 Nights
